# Teaching Philosophy

Teaching is an integral part of scientific research and innovation. My objective is to develop a strong conceptual understanding of physics while encouraging analytical thinking, computational skills, and research-oriented learning.

Students are motivated to explore modern topics in condensed matter physics, quantum mechanics, nanotechnology, and spintronics through classroom discussions, laboratory activities, and independent research projects.
