## Welcome to the Quantum Spintronics Lab

We are a research group dedicated to advancing our understanding of quantum spintronics and its applications in next-generation technologies.

### Our Research Focus

Our laboratory explores the quantum mechanical properties of electron spin and its applications in:

- **Spin Transport Phenomena**: Understanding and controlling spin currents in quantum materials
- **Quantum Devices**: Designing novel quantum devices exploiting spin-dependent effects
- **Topological Spintronics**: Exploring topological protection in spintronic systems
- **Quantum Computing Applications**: Leveraging spin qubits for quantum information processing
