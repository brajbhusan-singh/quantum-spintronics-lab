## Research Areas

### 1. Spin Transport and Dynamics

We investigate the fundamental mechanisms of spin transport in quantum materials, including:
- Spin Hall effects in novel materials
- Spin-orbit coupling phenomena
- Damping and relaxation mechanisms

### 2. Topological Spintronics

Exploring the interplay between topology and spintronics:
- Topological insulators and their spin properties
- Weyl semimetals and chiral transport
- Edge states and protected spin channels

### 3. Quantum Spin Qubits

Developing spin-based quantum bits for quantum information:
- Spin qubit coherence and control
- Quantum gate implementation
- Scaling towards quantum processors

### 4. Novel Materials and Heterostructures

Investigating emerging materials for spintronic applications:
- 2D materials (graphene, transition metal dichalcogenides)
- Magnetic heterostructures
- Engineered spin textures

### 5. Device Applications

Translating fundamental discoveries into practical applications:
- Spin-based sensors
- Quantum memories
- Novel computing paradigms
